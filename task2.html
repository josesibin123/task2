Delicious Pasta Recipe

Quick and easy homemade pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce, add salt & pepper.
  5. Top with parmesan and serve hot.
